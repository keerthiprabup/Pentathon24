Objective
The objective of the Hackathon is to develop products/solutions, relevant to the Indian Metro station environment in the AI/ML-based video analytics system.
Apply For
Professional Track
Apply For
Academic Track

Eligibility
Professional Track
  •    MNCs
  •    Indian Companies
  •    Startups
  •    Professionals
Academic Track
  •    Students across India

Students shall participate in team.
(minimum 2 to maximum 6 participants)
Note: At least one member of the team should be female.

Structure & Timeline

Registration

01 March to 26th March 2024

Registration and PPT Submission by Participants

Round 1

27,28 March 2024

Online/Offline Presentations and Screening

Round 2

30th March to 29th April 2024

Providing Dataset by Maha-Metro and Development Period for all Participants

Finals

2nd May to 3rd May 2024

Final Demonstration at Pune


Awards
Professional Track

The winner(s) will get the chance to demonstrate the solution in the live environment of Maharashtra Metro Rail Corporation Limited.

Academic Track

The winner(s) will be awarded with a cash prize of Rs. 25,000/- and may be given a golden opportunity to further develop and enhance the solution for Maharashtra Metro Rail Corporation Limited.

Evaluation Criteria

The product/solution(s) with the maximum applied working use cases specified as per tracks and minimum false alarms will be awarded as the winner(s).
